Park infomation

Welcome to the all-encompassing world of Sanjalika Water Park, where an array of exhilarating attractions and leisurely facilities await your discovery. Our Rides & Slides section is the epicenter of aquatic thrill, featuring 5 heart-pounding slides for adults and kids that twist and turn with excitement. This dynamic blend ensures a spectrum of aquatic experiences that cater to every age group, promising endless moments of laughter and joy.
For those seeking a more leisurely escape, our exclusive Ladies Pool Area is a sanctuary of relaxation. Crystal-clear pools, private cabanas, and attentive service converge to create a haven where women can unwind and rejuvenate amidst elegant surroundings. The meticulous design ensures an atmosphere of serenity, making it the perfect retreat for a tranquil day by the water.

As you explore our park, be sure to venture into our Horse Riding Area, an enchanting space where equestrian enthusiasts and novices alike can enjoy guided rides through scenic trails. The rhythmic hoofbeats create a harmonious connection with nature, offering a unique and serene experience. For those desiring a touch of the exotic, our Artificial Sea beckons. Immerse yourself in the therapeutic embrace of the sea without leaving the park, and let the gentle waves wash away your worries. Sports enthusiasts will find their haven in our Tennis Court and Cricket Play Area, where friendly competition and skill development take center stage. With well-maintained facilities and equipment, these areas cater to both amateurs and seasoned players.

At Sanjalika Water Park, we understand that timing is crucial for a perfect day out. That's why our park welcomes guests from morning until evening, ensuring ample time for families, friends, and individuals to revel in the vibrant atmosphere and make lasting memories. Come, be a part of the Sanjalika experience a world where the thrill of adventure meets the serenity of leisure, creating a perfect blend for a day filled with fun and relaxation.

About Us
