Food Zone

Indulge your taste buds in a culinary journey at the Food Zone of Sanjalika Water Park, where delicious flavors and vibrant ambiance come together to enhance your aquatic experience. Our Food Zone is designed to cater to a variety of tastes, offering a diverse selection of mouth-watering options to satisfy every craving.

Diverse Cuisine

Embark on a gastronomic adventure with our diverse range of cuisine options. From delectable local favorites to international delights, our Food Zone is a culinary melting pot that ensures there's something for every palate.

Our Dishes:
  • Mango Tango Salad:

    A refreshing salad that blends the sweetness of ripe mangoes with the tanginess of cherry tomatoes, all tossed in a zesty lime vinaigrette. This vibrant dish also features crisp cucumber, red onion, and a sprinkle of fresh cilantro for added depth of flavor.


  • Spicy Grilled Shrimp Tacos:

    Experience a flavor explosion with our Spicy Grilled Shrimp Tacos. Succulent shrimp are marinated in a blend of bold spices, then char-grilled to perfection. Served in warm corn tortillas and topped with crunchy cabbage slaw, fresh cilantro, and a drizzle of chipotle mayo.

Cafes and Snack Stations

Enjoy a quick bite or a refreshing beverage at our cozy cafes and snack stations strategically placed throughout the park. Whether you crave a hot cup of coffee, a sweet treat, or a savory snack, our convenient stations have you covered.

Our Dishes
  • Spicy Nacho Crunch:

    Embark on a spicy adventure with our Spicy Nacho Crunch, featuring crisp nacho chips drizzled with a fiery blend of spices, creating an explosion of bold flavors in every bite.


  • Loaded Potato Skins:

    Indulge in the savory goodness of our Loaded Potato Skins, where perfectly baked potatoes are filled with a tantalizing mix of cheese, bacon, and green onions, delivering a satisfying snack experience.

Refreshing Beverage Oasis

Quench your thirst at our Refreshing Beverage Oasis, where an array of refreshing drinks awaits. From fruit-infused coolers to classic sodas, this oasis is the perfect pitstop to recharge and stay hydrated.

Our Dishes
  • Tropical Bliss Smoothie:

    Quench your thirst with the Tropical Bliss Smoothie, a refreshing blend of tropical fruits that promises to cool you down on a warm day.


  • Minty Lemonade Refresher:

    Experience a burst of freshness with our Minty Lemonade Refresher, a zesty concoction of lemons and mint that revitalizes your senses.

Health-conscious Corner

For those with a focus on wellness, our Health-conscious Corner offers a selection of nutritious and wholesome options. From fresh salads to smoothie bowls, prioritize your well-being without compromising on flavor.

Our Dishes
  • Quinoa and Kale Power Bowl:

    Immerse yourself in a bowl of nutrient-rich goodness with our Quinoa and Kale Power Bowl. Fluffy quinoa is paired with vibrant kale, cherry tomatoes, roasted sweet potatoes, and avocado slices. Drizzled with a lemon-tahini dressing, this dish is a celebration of healthful flavors.


  • Acai Berry Smoothie Bowl:

    Dive into a bowl of antioxidant-rich delight with our Acai Berry Smoothie Bowl. A velvety blend of frozen acai berries, banana, and almond milk forms the base, topped with a colorful array of fresh berries, granola, and a sprinkle of chia seeds.

Family-Friendly Dining Areas

Relax and refuel with your loved ones in our family-friendly dining areas. With comfortable seating and a welcoming atmosphere, these spaces provide the perfect backdrop for enjoying a meal together.

Our Facilities
  • Welcoming Atmosphere:

    Our Family-Friendly Dining Areas exude a warm and welcoming ambiance, creating a comfortable space for families to gather and enjoy a meal together. The decor is thoughtfully designed to cater to all age groups, incorporating vibrant colors, playful elements, and spacious seating arrangements to accommodate families of different sizes.


  • Kid-Friendly Menu Options:

    Understanding the varying tastes and preferences of young diners, our Family-Friendly Dining Areas offer a diverse and kid-friendly menu. From nutritious and tasty options to familiar comfort foods, the menu caters to the discerning palates of children, ensuring that family dining is a delightful experience for both parents and kids.

  • Entertainment and Ample Space:

    To enhance the family dining experience, our dining areas provide ample space for families to spread out comfortably. Entertainment elements, such as play areas, interactive zones, or themed decor, are strategically incorporated to keep children engaged and entertained, allowing parents to enjoy a relaxed meal knowing their little ones are having fun in a safe environment.

Scenic Outdoor Seating:

Immerse yourself in the park's natural beauty while dining in our scenic outdoor seating areas. Surrounded by lush greenery and the sounds of water, these spaces offer a tranquil setting to savor your favorite dishes.

Our Facilities
  • Breathtaking Views:

    Scenic Outdoor Seating at our establishment offers diners breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. Whether it's a picturesque garden, a tranquil water feature, or expansive natural scenery, our outdoor seating is strategically positioned to provide a visual feast, creating a dining experience that extends beyond the plate.


  • Natural Ambiance and Fresh Air:

    One of the standout qualities of our Scenic Outdoor Seating is the opportunity for diners to immerse themselves in a natural ambiance. Surrounded by the beauty of the outdoors, guests can enjoy the refreshing sensation of fresh air, creating a serene and uplifting atmosphere that enhances the overall dining experience.


  • Al Fresco Dining Experience:

    Our Scenic Outdoor Seating is curated to offer an authentic al fresco dining experience. Guests have the opportunity to dine under the open sky, savoring their meals in the midst of nature. The setup is complemented by comfortable seating arrangements, allowing patrons to appreciate not only the delicious cuisine but also the delightful outdoor ambiance.
